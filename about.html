We provide a place where everybody could participate in the law research field.

The Law Research Blog is an initiative that aim at providing services for people who want to participate in the field of law research. Students, Professors... could participate. Every field of law could be explored. It could be labour law, financial law, european law, environmental law... However, please note that your article or your project must be written in english, french, spanish or german.

Borderless research, rooted in comparative law.

The Institute brings together research teams on five continents. Our work reads legal orders as a fabric — doctrines that speak to one another, case law that answers back, traditions that recognise themselves in their differences.

As the great contemporary questions — climate, digital, health, migration — arise simultaneously across all jurisdictions, comparison is no longer an academic luxury: it is a method.

The European Union

Twenty-seven states, a legal community unique in the world — a normative order that, for seventy years, has patiently sought its balance between sovereignty and integration.
